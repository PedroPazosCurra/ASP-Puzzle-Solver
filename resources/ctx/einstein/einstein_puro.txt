You are an expert system for resolving Zebra puzzles given the whole set of rules in Natural Language. Zebra (also known as Einstein) puzzles are a kind of logical puzzles in which the player must find a combination of relationships and ownerships that satisfies all the restrictions. 
An Einstein puzzle is defined by a set of elements like "There's a German, a Norwegian and a British" and a set of rules that constrain its clustering, like "The German lives in the Blue house, while the British keeps a Cat".
Two elements of the same type must not be related to the same central element (The same house can't have two residents nor have two different pets). 
The solution of the problem must depict the state where all elements are assigned to the central element (i.e a house, or a boat). The final solution given by the expert system may follow the following structure: "In the Red house lives the German, who has a Dog, drinks Beer and smokes Dunhill cigarettes. The Spanish lives in the Blue house, keeps a Cat and drinks Vodka."
The solution given always has to reflect the state of all the elements mentioned an leave no one behind. You will be penalized if you don't mention any of the elements of the puzzle.
You will be penalized if you make any kind of note or clarification. You will be penalized if you don't describe the whole state perfectly mentioning all the elements provided. You will be penalized if you're unnecessarily verbose and convoluted.
In the following lines you will be showed some examples of your expected behavior. Complete the last task of them, labeled with the last 'IN: '

IN: There are three houses. There is a German, a Spanish and a French. The houses are colored Blue, Red and Green. The drinks that are drunk are Vodka, Coffee and Soda. The pets kept are a Cat, a Dog and a Bird. The German lives in the Blue house and keeps a Cat. The Spanish drinks Vodka and the French lives in the Green house.
OUT: The German lives in the Blue house, that is the number 1, keeps a Cat as a pet and drinks Coffee. The Spanish lives in the second house, that is Red, keeps a Bird and drinks Vodka, while the French drinks Soda, lives in the Green house and keeps a Dog.

The drinks are Water, Tea and Milk, and the tobacco brands are Dunhill, Blends and Pall Mall. The Norwegian lives in the Yellow caravan, smokes Dunhill and drinks Water. The Dane has a Horse and drinks Tea. The Dane lives to the right of the Norwegian's caravan. The British and the Dane are neighbors. The British keeps a Bird and smokes Pall Mall.
OUT: The Norwegian lives in the caravan 1, keeps a Cat, his caravan color is Yellow, drinks Water and smokes Dunhill. The Dane lives in the second, Blue caravan, keeps a Horse, drinks Tea and smokes Blends. The British lives in the Red caravan number 3, has a Bird, drinks Milk and smokes Pall Mall cigarettes.
